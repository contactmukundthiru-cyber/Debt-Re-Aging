Common Questions

Is my financial data safe?
Yes. This tool was designed with a "privacy-first" architecture. Whether you use the Web App or the Desktop Suite, all processing happens locally on your device. No data is ever uploaded to a server or stored in the cloud.
How accurate is the analysis?
The tool uses 20+ automated rules based on FCRA and FDCPA regulations. While highly accurate for detecting timeline inconsistencies, it is designed to assist human review, not replace it. Always verify the results before sending disputes.
What types of errors does it detect?
It specializes in detecting "debt re-aging" (when collectors illegally refresh the 7-year reporting clock), statute of limitations violations, duplicate reporting by multiple collectors, and medical debt reporting violations.
Does the web app work offline?
Yes. After the first load, the web app runs fully offline and processes everything locally on your device.
What should I upload?
Upload a PDF, image, or paste the account section from your credit report. Scanned PDFs will run OCR automatically.
