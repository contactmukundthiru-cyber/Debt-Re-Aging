Detection

Transparent Rules

Every flag is based on documented, auditable rules. No black boxes.

A1

Excessive Reporting Period

Removal date exceeds 8 years from date opened

A2

DOFD Misalignment

Removal date inconsistent with DOFD + 7 years

B1

Classic Re-Aging

Date opened 24+ months after DOFD

B2

Missing DOFD

No DOFD on collection with recent open date

C1

Cross-Bureau Inconsistency

Different removal dates across bureaus

D1

SOL Expired

Account reported after statute of limitations

D2

Impossible Timeline

DOFD predates account open date

D3

Ancient Account

Account age exceeds 15 years

E1

Balance Irregularity

Balance increased after charge-off date

About

About This Project

The Debt Re-Aging Case Factory was created to address a critical gap in credit reporting enforcement. Debt re-aging is an illegal practice where collectors manipulate dates to extend how long negative information appears on credit reports.

This tool helps consumer advocates quickly identify potential violations by analyzing timeline inconsistencies in credit report data. It's designed for legal aid organizations, pro bono clinics, and consumer advocacy groups.

Important: This tool is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Always consult with a qualified attorney for legal matters.
