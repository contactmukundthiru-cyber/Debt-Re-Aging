Detection Engine

Transparent, Auditable Rules

Every flag is based on documented rules. No black boxes, no hidden logic.

A1

Excessive Reporting Period

Removal date exceeds 8 years from date opened

A2

DOFD Misalignment

Removal date inconsistent with DOFD + 7 years

B1

Classic Re-Aging

Date opened 24+ months after DOFD

B2

Missing DOFD

No DOFD on collection with recent open date

C1

Cross-Bureau Inconsistency

Different removal dates across bureaus

D1

SOL Expired

Account reported after statute of limitations

D2

Impossible Timeline

DOFD predates account open date

D3

Ancient Account

Account age exceeds 15 years

E1

Balance Irregularity

Balance increased after charge-off date

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is debt re-aging?

Debt re-aging is an illegal practice where collectors manipulate dates to extend how long negative information appears on credit reports, violating the Fair Credit Reporting Act.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All processing happens locally on your machine. No data is ever sent to external servers. The tool works completely offline after installation.

Who is this tool for?

This tool is designed for legal aid organizations, pro bono clinics, and consumer advocacy groups helping clients with credit report disputes.

Is this legal advice?

No. This tool is for informational purposes only. Always consult with a qualified attorney before taking legal action based on the findings.

What file formats are supported?

The tool accepts PDF, PNG, JPG, and JPEG files. It uses OCR to extract text from scanned documents and images.

How accurate is the detection?

All 9 detection rules are transparent and documented. You can review and verify each flag before generating dispute letters.
